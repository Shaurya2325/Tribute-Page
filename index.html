Sundar Pichai - CEO of Google

sundar Pichai is the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC. Born in India, he pursued engineering at IIT Kharagpur and then completedhis higher education in the U.S. at Stanford University and the WhartonSchool of the University of Pennsylvania.

known for his calm demeanor and sharp intellect,Sundar played a key role in the department of Google Chrome, and gradually rose through the ranks to become one of the most influential technology leaders in the world. under his leadership, Google has expanded into artificial intelligence, hardware, and other advanced technologies.
